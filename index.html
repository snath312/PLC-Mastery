COURSE HIGHLIGHTS

7 Easy Steps for PLC Mastery

  • STEP 1: Basics of Passive Electrical Elements & Digital Electronics
    • Basics of Passive Electrical - Foundation of Electrical System.
    • D Type Components, A-Type Components and T-Type Components in detail.
    • Binary & Decimal Numbers & their Conversion.
    • Complete knowledge of Basic Electrical Circuits.
    • Bits and Bytes.
    • A/D Converter.
    • Data Masking.
  • STEP 2: Understanding Field Devices & Sensors - Analog & Digital
    • Interfacing of Analog Input/Output Signal - Voltage & Current.
    • Interfacing of Digital Input/Output Sensors.
    • Scaling of Analog Input/Output.
    • Complete working principles of the Analog/Digital Sensors.
  • STEP 3: Basics of PLC hardware and Architecture
    • Architecture of PLC Setup.
    • Various Parts of the PLC in Details.
    • Signal Flow inside the PLC.
    • Safety devices used in the PLC.
    • PLC Communications.
  • STEP 4: PLC Ladder Programming and SCADA
    • Detailed Overview of PLC Ladder Instructions.
    • Types of Ladder Programming.
    • Creation of Ladder Logic as per condition from Scratch.
    • Basic Protocols for creating Ladder Logics.
    • SCADA and its applications.
  • STEP 5: Pneumatics and Hydraulic Interfacing
    • Application of Pneumatics & Hydraulics in Machine Tools and Process Plant.
    • Interfacing of PLC with Pneumatic System.
    • Interfacing of PLC with Hydraulic system.
    • Detailed Overview of Hydraulics & Pneumatics Circuits with Symbols.
    • Realization of Ladder logic and Physical Components.
  • STEP 6: Basics of Electrical Drawings and Schematics
    • Electrical Symbols.
    • How to read the Electrical Drawings of any Electrical & PLC Panel and troubleshoot easily.
    • How to create the Electrical Drawing for New PLC Setups.
  • STEP 7: Troubleshoot Mastery in the Field
    • Module Failure in the Input/Output and Field Devices.
    • Electrical Noise Interference.
    • Corrupted Memory.
    • Power Problems.
    • Communication Issues
    • You will Learn Strategies to find the fault in minimum time.
    • I will share all my tricks and techniques of troubleshooting collected the in last 29 years.
    • You will have ready solutions for your troubleshooting that will make your life easy.
    • How to use Various Instruments – Like Multimeter, Current Meter, Megger etc. for troubleshooting.
